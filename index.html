THROUGH TIME

  
 A Journey Across Human Development 
  
 Knowledge, Culture and Society Through Time 
  
    
  

 Through Time is a bilingual interdisciplinary digital museum created by students and bilingual teachers of IES Dama de Marfil. 

  

 The museum explores the development of human societies, cultures, knowledge and ways of life from Prehistory to the Contemporary World through History, Biology and Geology, Geography, English, French, Physical Education and Spanish Language and Literature.
